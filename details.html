How we determine the air quality at your chosen destination.

CatchMyBreath cares about your health and the health of our world so in order to do our part we created a way for you to check the air quality at the destination that you're thinking about visiting. The way we do this is by utilizing the (API) Air Pollution concept which collects data such as Carbon monoxide (CO), Nitrogen monoxide (NO), Nitrogen dioxide (NO2), Ozone (O3), Sulphur dioxide (SO2) and many other gases to determine the air quality wherever you choose to go.


The Juicy stuff

Air pollution can be measured in different ways such as Air quality monitors, lasers to scan the density of the air, and also satellite imaging. We take that information and insert it into our database so that when you, the user, input a destination, the air quality in that area comes up, allowing you to make an educated decision whether to travel based on your health and other determining factors.

Air Pollution - OpenWeatherMap Air Pollution API provides current, forecast and historical air pollution data for any coordinates on the globe. Besides basic Air Quality Index, the API returns data about polluting gases, such as Carbon monoxide (CO), Nitrogen monoxide (NO), Nitrogen dioxide (NO2), Ozone (O3), Sulphur dioxide (SO2), Ammonia (NH3), and particulates (PM2.5 and PM10).
