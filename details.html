How we determine the air quality at your chosen destination.

CatchMyBreathe cares about your health and the health of our world so in order to do our part we created a way for you to check the air quality at the destination that your thinking about visiting. The way we do this is by utilizing the (API) Air Pollution concept which collects data such as Carbon monoxide (CO), Nitrogen monoxide (NO), Nitrogen dioxide (NO2), Ozone (O3), Sulphur dioxide (SO2) and many other gases to determine the air quality wherever you choose to go.





The Juicy stuff

Air pollution can be measured in different ways such as Air quality monitors, lasers to scan the density of the air, and also sattelite imaging. We take that information an insert it into our data base so that when you the user inputs a destination the air quality in that area comes up allowing you to make an educated decision whether to travel based on your health and other determining factors.
